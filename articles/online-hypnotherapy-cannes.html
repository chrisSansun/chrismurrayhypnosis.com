What Online Hypnotherapy Addresses

Cognitive hypnotherapy works with the way your mind actually works, not against it. In Cannes, I work with clients on anxiety that arrives without warning, confidence gaps despite outward success, sleep disruption from work stress, and the particular challenge of managing high performance while staying grounded.

The approach is collaborative. You're not being put under, not losing control, not following someone else's narrative. Instead, we map the patterns keeping you stuck, we understand the function those patterns serve, and then we shift them at the level where they actually live - in your mind's habit loops and learned responses. This isn't suggestion-based hypnosis. It's practical neuroscience applied.

Many professionals arrive thinking hypnotherapy is either magic or nonsense. Neither. It's a measurable process where your mind is actively engaged in rewiring its own responses. Hypnotherapy for confidence focuses this work specifically on dismantling the self-doubt patterns that undermine performance. It's not about feeling better temporarily. It's about training your nervous system to respond differently to pressure and uncertainty.

How Online Hypnotherapy Sessions Work

A typical first session is 60 minutes. We spend that time understanding what's actually happening - not what you think should be happening, but what you experience moment to moment. I ask questions that help you become more aware of the exact trigger, the automatic response, the consequence. Most people aren't used to examining their own patterns this closely, and that awareness itself starts the process of change.

Subsequent sessions build on that foundation. Some sessions are lighter on the hypnotherapy component and heavier on strategy - we're solving the puzzle of how you got here and what needs to shift. Other sessions lean into the hypnotic process itself, where your conscious mind steps back a bit and we work more directly with the deeper patterns your unconscious mind is holding.

The technology is straightforward. You need a reliable internet connection, a quiet private space, and a device with video and audio. Everything is encrypted. No recordings unless you specifically ask, and that's all handled with full consent. The session feels less formal than a traditional office visit, but it's just as professional and just as effective. For many clients, especially those traveling frequently or managing expat life, the convenience and flexibility of online work is actually preferable.
