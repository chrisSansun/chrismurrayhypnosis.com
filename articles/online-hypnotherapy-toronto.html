What Cognitive Hypnotherapy Actually Does

Cognitive hypnotherapy isn't stage hypnosis or someone swinging a pocket watch. It's a clinical approach that combines hypnosis with cognitive principles to identify and reframe the thinking patterns that fuel your problem. If you're dealing with anxiety, procrastination, perfectionism, or performance blocks, the root issue usually isn't willpower or discipline. It's an old pattern your brain learned to protect you, and that pattern no longer serves you.

During a session, you'll be fully conscious and in control. You're not unconscious or weak-willed. Instead, you reach a state of focused attention where your unconscious mind becomes accessible and responsive. That's when real change becomes possible. I work with your brain's natural problem-solving systems to update these patterns. If you want to understand this more deeply, learning about what cognitive hypnotherapy actually is will give you the full picture. The process is collaborative, specific, and measurable. You'll know within 3-4 sessions whether it's working.
