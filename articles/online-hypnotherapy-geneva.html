What cognitive hypnotherapy actually does

Cognitive hypnotherapy combines talking therapy with hypnotic trance, which means we're not just discussing your issues - we're actually changing the patterns underneath them. In trance, your conscious mind takes a step back. That's not weakness or loss of control. It's the opposite - it's the only state where you can actually reach the parts of your mind that are driving the unhelpful patterns.

The cognitive part matters enormously. We don't use hypnotherapy to "relax problems away" or to impose suggestions onto you. Instead, we identify the exact thinking patterns maintaining your anxiety, perfectionism, or indecision, then use hypnosis to help you shift those patterns at a level where they actually live - in your unconscious mind.

With executive clients, this often means addressing the internal dialogue that tells you you're never quite performing well enough, or that every decision carries catastrophic weight. We work with perfectionism, analysis paralysis, and the particular burnout that comes from always being "on". The changes tend to be durable because we're not fighting against your mind - we're helping it work in ways that actually serve you.

How online sessions work from Geneva

I run sessions via secure video call, which is functionally identical to in-person work. You need a quiet space and a reliable internet connection - that's it. No need to travel, no waiting room, no disruption to your day beyond the session itself. Sessions are 50 minutes, and we typically schedule weekly or bi-weekly depending on your situation.

The first session is always consultation and information gathering. I'll ask about what's brought you to hypnotherapy, what you've already tried, and what success would actually look like for you. That clarity matters - it shapes everything that follows. We talk through how hypnosis works, you'll experience what trance actually feels like, and we'll establish the specific focus for your treatment.

Subsequent sessions build on that foundation. We're working toward measurable change - reduced anxiety in specific situations, clearer decision-making, better sleep, whatever you came for. Most of my Geneva clients see meaningful shifts within 4-6 sessions, though everyone's different.
