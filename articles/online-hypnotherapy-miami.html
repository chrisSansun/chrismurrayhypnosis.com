What Cognitive Hypnotherapy Can Address

I specialise in cognitive hypnotherapy, which combines hypnotic techniques with cognitive behavioural principles. This means we're not just suggesting positive thoughts. We're identifying the specific thoughts and beliefs driving your behaviour, then using hypnosis to help your mind absorb a different framework. Many clients come to me with anxiety that's been triggered by past experiences or perfectionist standards. Others want to build genuine confidence before presentations, negotiations, or major life transitions.

Whether you're managing work stress, dealing with public speaking anxiety, working through relationship patterns, or recovering from setbacks, the same principle applies. Your brain's got habits. Hypnosis is an efficient way to update them. The science backs this up. Clinical hypnosis activates different neural pathways than normal waking consciousness, making it possible to establish new associations and responses.

How Online Sessions Actually Work

An online hypnotherapy session starts like a conversation. We spend time understanding what's brought you here, what you've already tried, and what matters most to you. Then I'll guide you into a focused, deeply relaxed state using techniques tailored to your learning style. This isn't the swinging pocket watch from films. It's simply directing your attention inward so your conscious mind settles and your unconscious mind becomes more receptive.

During this focused state, you'll hear suggestions and ideas that address the root of what you came for. You remain in control throughout. You're not asleep. You can hear everything, you can move if you need to, and you can come out of the state anytime. After we finish the hypnotic portion, we'll talk through what you experienced and how to carry this forward. Understanding how hypnosis actually reshapes your neural responses helps you stay committed between sessions and notice the changes as they happen.
