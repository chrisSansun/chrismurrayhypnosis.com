How the Online Session Process Works

Your first session is an intake - we discuss what's brought you to hypnotherapy, what you want to change, and how hypnosis actually works in practice. A lot of people come to me saying they don't really believe in hypnosis. That's fine. Belief isn't a prerequisite. What matters is whether you're willing to pay attention for an hour. From there, we establish a clear goal and I guide you into a state of focused concentration where your unconscious mind becomes more receptive to constructive suggestion. You're not asleep, not under anyone's control - you're simply in a state of heightened attention, and you'll remember everything that happens. Subsequent sessions build on the work we've started, introducing specific cognitive techniques and imagery tailored to your situation. Most clients work with me for 6-12 sessions depending on their goals and how quickly the changes take hold.
