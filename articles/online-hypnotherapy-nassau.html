How Online Hypnotherapy Actually Works

Cognitive hypnotherapy is different from the stage show version most people have seen. You're not asleep. You're not out of control. What happens is that your conscious mind relaxes while your working memory becomes more receptive to new patterns and insights. We use that state to help you reframe the beliefs and habits that drive your anxiety, avoidance, or other challenges.

How online hypnotherapy works is straightforward: we talk through what's happening, I guide you into a focused state, and we do the work. You'll hear everything I say. You're in control. Most people describe it as deeply calm but clear-headed. Afterwards, you'll have concrete shifts in how you're thinking about the problem. That's where real change begins.
